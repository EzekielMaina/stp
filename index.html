About US

Established in 2016, Springtide Properties Limited is accredited to the Institution of Surveyors of Kenya (ISK), Valuation and Estate Management (VEMS) Chapter and licensed by the Estate Agents Registration Board (EARB) to offer Estate Agency Services everywhere in Kenya as per the Estate Agency Act Cap 533 of the Laws of Kenya.

For more than 8 years, Springtide has undertaken numerous assignments and offered in-depth professional advice on Estate Agency, Property Valuation, Feasibility Studies, project management, Property and Facilities Management.

Our staff uphold very high professional ethics and are well trained in all facets of the real estate industry ready to serve you.
